Our History

Our History

Farm to Flame Energy Inc.

...was founded in July of 2018 after five years of preparation by co founder, Will McKnight. He became familiar with the combustion process after working with Summerhill Biomass Systems Inc. to develop and research it. He saw its potential for remote power generation and, along with classmate Kwaku Jyamfi, started an independent company to further develop the combustion process and use it in real-world applications.
