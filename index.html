Reinventing Fire

Reinventing Fire

Our unique and proprietary combustion process provides us with the means of making biomass burn like a gas.

Our unique and proprietary combustion process provides us with the means of making biomass burn like a gas.

Smokeless

Odorless

Emission free

This revolutionary combustion process burns various forms of biowaste (reduced to fine powder) in place of fossil fuels. This fuel burns as intensely as traditional fuel sources, while being safe for the environment.

This process has many benefits, including:

  • A controlled flame
  • Simple thermostat controls
  • Easy retrofit to existing burners
